Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a custom order?

Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity and current workload. Once you submit your request, Tasha will contact you to discuss the timeline and confirm the details.

What materials do you use?

We use high-quality, hypoallergenic yarn that is soft, durable, and safe for babies and sensitive skin. All materials are sourced from trusted suppliers to ensure the best quality for your handmade pieces.

Do you offer shipping?

Yes! We ship nationwide. Shipping costs and delivery times will be discussed after your order is confirmed. We carefully package each item to ensure it arrives in perfect condition.

Can I request a specific color or design?

Absolutely! Custom orders are all about YOUR vision. We can work with any color, design, or size you request. Just fill out the custom order form and let us know your preferences in detail.

How do I care for my crochet items?

Hand wash in cool water with mild soap. Avoid bleach and harsh chemicals. Lay flat to dry. With proper care, your handmade crochet pieces will last for years to come!
